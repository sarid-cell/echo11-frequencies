What Data We Collect
🔒 No registration. No personal data we ask you for.

echo.11 does not require registration, login, account creation, or any personal information from you. The app runs entirely in your browser.

echo.11 itself does not collect:

Names, emails, or contact information
Health or biometric data
Precise location data
Audio recordings or microphone input

However, two third-party services we use (Google Analytics and Vercel) collect limited technical and usage data — see "Third-Party Services" below. You can opt out at any time.

Local Storage

Your preferences (dark/light mode, language, volume) are stored locally in your browser only. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Audio Processing

All audio is generated locally in your browser using the Web Audio API. No audio is recorded, transmitted, or stored.

Third-Party Services
Google Analytics 4 — Property G-KSCG5KS848. Collects anonymized usage events (pages viewed, frequencies played, session duration), a pseudonymous client ID stored as a cookie, and a truncated IP address. We do not enable Google Signals or ad personalization. You can opt out below.
Ko-fi — When you click a Support button, a new tab opens at ko-fi.com/echo11. Ko-fi (and its payment processor PayPal) handle all payment information independently. echo.11 never sees your card or PayPal details.
Vercel — Our hosting provider may log standard server access logs (IP address, timestamp, user agent) for security and performance.
Google Fonts — We load fonts from fonts.googleapis.com. Google's privacy policy applies.
Cookies We Use

echo.11 itself sets no cookies. Google Analytics sets a _ga cookie (pseudonymous client identifier, expires after 2 years) and one or more _ga_KSCG5KS848 cookies (session state). If you opt out (see below), these cookies are not used to send data.

Data Retention
Google Analytics events: retained for 14 months, then deleted automatically.
Vercel server logs: typically retained for ~30 days.
Local storage (your browser): stays on your device until you clear your browser data.
Children's Privacy

echo.11 is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the right to:

Access — request a copy of any data linked to your browser. Email us; we will work with Google to retrieve what's available.
Delete — request deletion of your analytics record. We will submit the request to Google Analytics within 30 days.
Opt out — disable analytics tracking on this browser, immediately and at any time, using the button below.
Withdraw consent — same as opt out. Your choice takes effect on the next page load.

For access or deletion requests, email echo11frequencies@gmail.com. We respond within 5 business days.

Manage Analytics on This Browser

You can disable Google Analytics tracking for echo.11 on this browser. The setting is stored locally and persists across visits.

Analytics: enabled (default)
