What Data We Collect
🔒 We collect NO personal data

echo.11 does not require registration, login, or any personal information. The app runs entirely in your browser.

We do not collect:

Names, emails, or contact information
Health or biometric data
Location data
Usage analytics or cookies
Local Storage

Your preferences (dark/light mode, language, volume) are stored locally in your browser only. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Audio Processing

All audio is generated locally in your browser using the Web Audio API. No audio is recorded, transmitted, or stored.

Third-Party Services
Google Fonts — We load fonts from fonts.googleapis.com. Google's privacy policy applies.
Vercel — Our hosting provider may log standard server access logs (IP address, timestamp).
Children's Privacy

echo.11 is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
